The impact of Trump’s election on the stock market

Tariff Policy and Market Volatility

In 2025, President Donald Trump’s actions before and after his inauguration had a significant effect on the U.S. stock market. Among these, his tariff and trade policies created the most immediate volatility, while economic stimulus and deregulation also played important roles.

  • Tariff Policy Opened in Liberation Day

    • On April 2, 2025, Trump announced tariffs of 10% on most imported goods (“Liberation Day”), causing a sharp market reaction. The S&P 500 fell 3.15%, and the Nasdaq dropped over 10% in a single day

  • President Trump's Tariff Reversal

    • S&P 500 jumped about 9.5% immediately reflecting renewed optimism
